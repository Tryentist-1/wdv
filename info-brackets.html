How Brackets Work

Solo Team

Quick reference for coaches, archers, and spectators. Mobile-friendly and printable.

Solo Swiss Brackets

1v1 head-to-head matches

Format

  • Two archers per match, head-to-head
  • 5 sets maximum, 3 arrows per set
  • Win set: 2 points · Tie: 1 point each
  • First to 6 set points wins
  • Shoot-off if tied 5–5 after 5 sets

Bale Layout

Each bale has 4 targets (A, B, C, D) in two lines. Line 1 shoots first, then Line 2.

Line 1: Archer1 (Target A) vs Archer2 (Target B)
Line 2: Archer3 (Target C) vs Archer4 (Target D)

Two solo matches per bale (one per line).

Archer Flow

  1. Check Home Screen for opponent, bale, and target
  2. Go to your bale and target (A, B, C, or D)
  3. Line 1 shoots first; Line 2 shoots after
  4. Score all 5 sets; tap Complete Match when done
  5. Coach verifies; next round assignments appear after verification

Swiss Pairing

Winners face winners, losers face losers. No repeat opponents when possible. Divisions: Boys/Girls Varsity (BVAR/GVAR), Boys/Girls JV (BJV/GJV).

Team Swiss Brackets

3v3 team matches

Format

  • Two teams of 3 archers each
  • 4 sets maximum
  • Each archer shoots 2 arrows per set (6 arrows per team per set)
  • Archers shoot one at a time: Archer 1 → Archer 2 → Archer 3
  • Win set: 2 points · Tie: 1 point each
  • First team to 5 set points wins
  • Shoot-off if tied 4–4 after 4 sets

Bale Layout

Each team shares one target. All 3 archers take turns on that target.

Line 1: Team1 (Target A) vs Team2 (Target B)
Line 2: Team3 (Target C) vs Team4 (Target D)

Two team matches per bale (one per line).

Team Flow

  1. Check Home Screen for opponent team, bale, and target
  2. Meet your team at the assigned bale
  3. Line 1 shoots first; Line 2 shoots after
  4. Take turns: Archer 1 shoots 2, Archer 2 shoots 2, Archer 3 shoots 2
  5. Score all 4 sets; tap Complete Match when done
  6. Coach verifies; next round assignments appear after verification

Teams

Teams are School + Gender + Level (e.g., WDV Men's Varsity T1). Each team has exactly 3 archers. Cross-school "MIX" teams are allowed when a school doesn't have 3 archers.
